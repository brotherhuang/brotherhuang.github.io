About Me

I'm currently a Research Scientist at Megvii/Face++ Research. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UMass Amherst. During my Ph.D., I worked in the Computer Graphics Research Group with Prof. Evangelos Kalogerakis and Prof. Rui Wang. Before joining UMass Amherst, I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees in Department of Mathematics , Zhejiang University

My research focuses on visual content analysis and creation. I am interested in developing computer vision, computer graphics, and machine learning techniques that help people to easily create and manipulate 3D models of objects.
